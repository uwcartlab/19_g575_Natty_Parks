Roads and the National Parks

Mapping 900,000 geotagged photos to understand how we experience the national parks.

By Nick Underwood, Clare Sullivan, and Peter Newman

6.38 million people visited US national parks last year. But how many of them experienced wildness?

In an analysis of over 900,000 Flickr photos taken in national parks, only X% were taken Y distance from roads. If we take the locations of Flickr photos as a proxy for movement patterns in the parks, only Z visitors make it further than X distance from roads during their visit.
